About Me

Learning, Leading, Succeeding

Born into a legacy of entrepreneurial excellence, Mr. Vishnu R. G represents the third generation of a business family that has shaped the food industry in Kerala and beyond. The RG Group, founded in 1940 by Sri M. Gopalan, began its journey with a focus on producing premium-quality Gingelly oil—a product that quickly became a household name across the state. This foundation of trust and quality was later expanded by his father, Mr. Ramesh R. G, who strengthened the company’s operations and extended its reach throughout India.

Awards

Each award represents dedication, hard work, and a commitment to success.

Kerala State Business Excellence Award (2012)

The Kerala State Business Excellence Award was given to RG Group for its outstanding contribution in the industry.

This recognition threw a light on the company’s focus on quality, innovation and customer satisfaction, while at the same time making it a brand of trust in its segment.

Young Chamber of Commerce – Product of the Year (2015)

In 2015, the Young Chamber of Commerce awarded RG Group with the award of product of the year for its outstanding innovation in the product and its market impact. This win was a tribute to the company's commitment to the delivery of products that are good quality and good for users.

Brand Achiever Award (2015)

RG Group was also awarded the Brand Achiever Award for its successful rebranding and expansion into new markets.

It gave credit to the company’s performance in merging a traditional approach with a business strategy and turning RG into a renowned and reputable name in the industry.

Young Entrepreneurs Award by Rotary Club Of Calicut City (2016)

Mr Vishnu R. G, the visionary of business approach, was given the Young entrepreneurs Award by the Rotary Club of Calicut City.With a deserving leadership in expanding RG Group globally, introducing new product lines, and keeping business excellence, he should receive this prestigious award.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the questions we get asked the most. Hopefully, you'll find the answers you're looking for!

Who is RG Group? And in what industries does it operate?

RG Group is a Kerala based, leading business group with an area of specialisation in largest Gingelly oil producer and exporter. In the span of years RG has expanded itself into various professions such as marine trade (RG Marines), export (Pride India Exports Pvt Ltd), food product (RG Foods), overseas acquisition (RG Links) and retail (RG Arcade).

What are the contributions of the current CEO of RG Group?

RG Group is headed by Mr. Vishnu R. G as CEO and Executive Director. He has set the pace in modernizing the brand, in spreading its global face, and acquiring ISO, GMP, and AGMARK certifications. While with RG Group, he introduced more than 30+ new product lines and made his presence felt in 18 countries.

In 2012 what was the meaning of RG Group’s rebranding?

The 2012 rebranding modelled the brand, packaging and marketing strategies of RG Group, and turned it into an internationally known brand. The new labelled launch of the RG Gingelly oil bottle at Westway Hotel, Calicut, offered a turning point and enhanced its appeal while retaining the centuries old tradition and quality image of the company.
