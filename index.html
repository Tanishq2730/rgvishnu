Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the questions we get asked the most. Hopefully, you'll find the answers you're looking for!

Who is RG Group? And in what industries does it operate?

RG Group is a Kerala based, leading business group with an area of specialisation in largest Gingelly oil producer and exporter. In the span of years RG has expanded itself into various professions such as marine trade (RG Marines), export (Pride India Exports Pvt Ltd), food product (RG Foods), overseas acquisition (RG Links) and retail (RG Arcade).

What are the contributions of the current CEO of RG Group?

RG Group is headed by Mr. Vishnu R. G as CEO and Executive Director. He has set the pace in modernizing the brand, in spreading its global face, and acquiring ISO, GMP, and AGMARK certifications. While with RG Group, he introduced more than 30+ new product lines and made his presence felt in 18 countries.

In 2012 what was the meaning of RG Group’s rebranding?

The 2012 rebranding modelled the brand, packaging and marketing strategies of RG Group, and turned it into an internationally known brand. The new labelled launch of the RG Gingelly oil bottle at Westway Hotel, Calicut, offered a turning point and enhanced its appeal while retaining the centuries old tradition and quality image of the company.
