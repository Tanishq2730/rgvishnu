About Me

UI/UX Designer & Web Developer

Mr. Vishnu R G was born into a family that had maintained deep roots in business success. Sri M. Gopalan founded RG Group during 1940, thus establishing a strong brand legacy which now includes trusted production of Gingelly oil throughout Kerala.

His father Mr. Ramesh R G continued this family business legacy by both strengthening operations and spreading his company throughout India.

Under the direction of Mr. Vishnu R G RG Group evolved into a contemporary international business that expanded its operations internationally.

Since childhood Vishnu possessed both a natural ability to assess business opportunities that would help the company grow and developed his business talents. The competitive market required RG Group to update its business model by combining innovative solutions with new market expansion strategies in order to succeed. Vishnu R G aimed to achieve business growth beyond basic sustainability by making the company future-proof and competitive within an advancing industry.

A New Era of RG Groups

Vishnu R. G launched a rebranding strategy with modernization plans to create a new era for RG Group.
When he took the position of CEO & Executive Director Vishnu R. G realized it was vital to modernize the company through maintaining core values from earlier leadership. The year 2012 saw him drive a total business rebrand that implemented an international brand identity to reflect the company's ongoing development.

At Westway Hotel in Calicut the introduction of the new RG Gingelly Oil bottle started an important phase for the company.
The redesign project provided more than visual improvements because it sought to improve operational performance constructively and adopt modern technology while expanding its product range.
New branding launched RG Group as a leading forward-thinking organization dedicated to customers and establishing itself in both domestic and international markets.

The certifications achieved under Vishnu’s leadership at RG Group included ISO, GMP and AGMARK which confirmed the company’s dedication to quality standards and safety measures along with superior customer satisfaction. The certifications improved brand trustworthiness and made international growth opportunities possible for the company.

Expansion into New Business Sectors

Vishnu R. G realized modernizing RG Group was not enough because he wanted to guide the company toward new business opportunities and market expansions. His strategic vision allowed him to expand RG Group into various business sectors which secured lasting growth and sustainability.

    Some of his key initiatives included
  • Pride India Exports Pvt Ltd – Expanding RG products across GCC countries.
  • RG Marines – Strengthening the company’s presence in the marine industry.
  • RG Foods – Introducing 30+ product lines in the food sector.
  • RG Links – A dedicated overseas procurement service provider.
  • RG Arcade – A modern shopping complex.

The strategic business expansion of RG Group created a diversified organization that now operates across 18+ countries through its extensive distribution network which numbers 200+ distributors throughout India.

A Vision for the Future:

The achievements of Mr. Vishnu R. G marks just the initial stages of his ongoing business path. His objective includes building RG Foods into a worldwide brand that will distribute authentic Indian products to consumers in all markets across the planet.

He expands business operations by pursuing new opportunities through his dedication to quality together with innovation and customer advocacy while preserving the core values of RG Group.

Mr. Vishnu R. G defines success through meaningful results rather than financial metrics or business growth. Through his leadership Mr. Vishnu R. G turned RG Group into a worldwide respected brand while simultaneously creating employment and fortifying commercial bonds and providing superior product quality to customers.

Mr. Vishnu R. G leads RG Group toward growth while staying devoted to delivering exceptional results and preserving valuable traditions.
