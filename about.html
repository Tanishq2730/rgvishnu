About Me

UI/UX Designer & Web Developer

Sri M. Gopalan founded the RG Group of Companies in 1940, which has been a trusted name in Kerala till date. What started as a budding company producing Gingelly oil has now become such a major producer and exporter of Gingelly oil in the region. Through the years, three generations of leadership have been visionaries, contributing a new dimension of success and innovation.

After Sri M. Gopalan, his son Mr. Ramesh RG continued to strengthen the foundation and expand the business. The company grew later under the guidance of Mr. Vishnu R. G, present CEO, Executive Director. He was a dynamic entrepreneur blessed with a sharp business mind, who brought up the brand by securing the ISO, GMP, and AGMARK certifications which guarantee quality to the most levels. Rebranding the company in 2012 has allowed him to modernize the company’s identity while maintaining its unwavering customer dedication.

RG Group adopted innovation as the main line of its development and the new business areas on which it focused were

  • Pride India Exports Pvt Ltd – Expanding RG products across GCC countries.
  • RG Marines – Strengthening the company’s presence in the marine industry.
  • RG Foods – Introducing 30+ product lines in the food sector.
  • RG Links – A dedicated overseas procurement service provider.
  • RG Arcade – A modern shopping complex.

RG Group is present in 18 countries today with a global network of more than 200+ distributors across India. RG Foods has a vision to be a part of the global market and hence is innovating and growing.

Today, RG Group has a presence in 18 countries and a strong network of 200+ distributors across India. With a vision to take RG Foods to a global market, the company continues to innovate and grow.

Mr. Vishnu R. G’s leadership and business excellence have been recognized with prestigious awards, including:

  • Kerala State Business Excellence Award (2012).
  • Young Chamber of Commerce – Product of the Year (2015).
  • Brand Achiever Award (2015).
  • Young Manager Award by Calicut Management Association (2016).
  • Young Entrepreneurs Award by Rotary Club Of Calicut City (2016).

Growing with each milestone, RG Group implements a tradition of quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction and looks forward to scale even higher.
