About Me

UI/UX Designer & Web Developer

The RG group of companies known today to be the largest producers and exporters of Gingelly oil in Kerala has seen three generations of eminent visionaries steering its course towards success.

Mr. Vishnu R G, a young entrepreneur with an extraordinary foresight and unmatched talents in marketing upgraded his grandfather's business to a brand new level. Mr. Vishnu’s efforts fetched the ISO/GMP and AGMARK certifications for the company. Under his leadership, the company furthered ventured into arenas like Pride India Exports Pvt Ltd with wide span in GCC countries, RG Marines, RG Foods, RG Links, the overseas procure- ment service provider, RG ARCADE, the shopping complex. Although always adhering to the traditional values incurred from his grandfather, Mr. Vishnu doesn’t compromise on employing state-of-the-art technology in his business and is always up to date with the latest trends in the market. He ideated, initiated and executed the company’s rebranding in the year 2012 that totally reformed the company and brought in all the more fortune, yet serving the Customers in better ways.
