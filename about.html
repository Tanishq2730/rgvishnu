About Me

Learning. Leading. Succeeding.

Born into a legacy of entrepreneurial excellence, Mr. Vishnu R. G represents the third generation of a business family that has shaped the food industry in Kerala and beyond. The RG Group, founded in 1940 by Sri M. Gopalan, began its journey with a focus on producing premium-quality Gingelly oil—a product that quickly became a household name across the state. This foundation of trust and quality was later expanded by his father, Mr. Ramesh R. G, who strengthened the company’s operations and extended its reach throughout India.

With a deep understanding of both heritage and modern business needs, Mr. Vishnu R. G, as CEO & Executive Director, has led RG Group into a new era—transforming it into a global enterprise rooted in values yet focused on innovation.

A Natural Entrepreneurial Instinct

From a young age, Vishnu demonstrated a keen sense for identifying opportunities and understanding market dynamics. With the business environment evolving rapidly, he recognized the importance of innovation, strategic agility, and global thinking. His goal wasn’t just to sustain RG Group—but to future-proof it and elevate it to a world-class brand.

A New Era for RG Group

In 2012, Mr. Vishnu R. G spearheaded a comprehensive rebranding and modernization initiative, redefining the company’s visual identity, operational framework, and market positioning. The unveiling of the redesigned RG Gingelly Oil bottle at Westway Hotel, Calicut, marked a symbolic beginning of this transformation.

This evolution was not just aesthetic—it reflected deeper strategic changes, such as the adoption of modern technologies, enhanced production efficiency, and an expanded product portfolio that aligned with global standards.

Under his leadership, RG Group achieved prestigious certifications including ISO, GMP, and AGMARK, reinforcing the company’s commitment to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction. These milestones elevated RG Group’s credibility and unlocked new international market opportunities.

Strategic Business Expansion

Recognizing the need for diversification, Mr. Vishnu R. G led RG Group’s expansion into new verticals and global markets. His strategic foresight resulted in the establishment of several key ventures:

  • Pride India Exports Pvt. Ltd. – Exporting high-quality RG products across the GCC and other international markets
  • RG Marines – Enhancing presence in the marine and seafood trade
  • RG Foods – Launching over 30+ product lines in the food and FMCG sector
  • RG Links – A specialized arm for overseas procurement and international partnerships
  • RG Arcade – A modern retail and shopping destination

Today, RG Group operates in 18+ countries, supported by a robust network of 200+ distributors across India and a growing global footprint.

A Vision for the Future:

For Mr. Vishnu R. G, success is not measured in numbers alone—it is defined by impact, purpose, and enduring value. His mission is to position RG Foods as a globally recognized brand, delivering authentic Indian Flavors to kitchens around the world.

His leadership continues to focus on innovation, integrity, and customer-centric growth—while always honoring the core values that shaped the RG Group’s legacy. Beyond building a business, he is committed to creating jobs, strengthening partnerships, and delivering exceptional quality to customers worldwide.

Legacy in Motion

Mr. Vishnu R G leads RG Group with a rare blend of traditional wisdom and modern vision—building a business that not only stands tall today but is also built for the future.

"True success lies in building something that outlasts you—something that uplifts people, adds value, and stands the test of time."
